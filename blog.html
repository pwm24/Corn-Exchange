What we sell

Cards
For every occasion

We stock a great selection of cards to meet your needs whether it is for a Birthday, anniversary, new home, congratulations, sympathy, engagement, wedding, new baby, thank you, good luck, Christmas, New Year, Easter, Valentines or relations card.

We buy our cards from high quality suppliers such as Woodmansterne, Paperlink and Tracks.

Gifts
Presents for all the family

We stock a huge selection of affordable presents for adults and children. Our gifts range from fine-bone China mugs and beautiful candles and lotions to children's books and toys. We also sell home accessories such as photo frames and cushions and fashion accessories in the form of handbags, scarves and jewellery.

We hand-pick the best products from out suppliers Jellycat, Churchill China, David Jones, Envy and many more.
